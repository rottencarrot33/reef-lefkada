Who we are

We are inspired by the view — relaxing, serene and welcoming.

At Reef, we've created a space that brings people together. Inspired by the beauty of Lefkada, our unique waterfront location and the warmth of Greek hospitality, we craft dishes that celebrate local ingredients and pair them with carefully curated cocktails in a vibrant atmosphere.

Whether you're a Lefkada resident, an expat, or a traveler, Reef is your home away from home.
